Good to Know

What's Not Included

🛋

Unfurnished Units

All units are delivered unfurnished. Any furniture shown in listing photos is used for staging purposes only and will not be present at move-in.

⚡

Utilities

Tenants are responsible for setting up their own electricity account through LADWP and arranging internet service with a provider of their choice.

🏢

Building Services Fee

A monthly building services fee is billed separately through Yes Energy. This covers shared building operating costs and will be outlined in your lease.

💳

Online Rent Payment

Rent is paid online through RentCafe — convenient, secure, and available 24/7 from any device.
